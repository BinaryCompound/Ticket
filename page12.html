my future profession

My future profession is teaching. It seems to me it is challenging and rewarding job. But also, it is very important and it makes a positive impact on society. Besides, there are many benefits to a teaching career. For example, healthcare and retirement benefits. They will be very useful in the future. And the most interesting benefits are extended vaccinations. In spite of these benefits, there can also be drawbacks to becoming a teacher. Teachers should have enough time to grade papers and homework. And sometimes, teachers may work overtime, but I believe that there is additional pay for this. Teachers should be patient and hardworking to complete the administrative tasks and meet deadlines. Also, it is important for teachers to engage students in the subject, that's why teachers should make a good lesson plan. In conclusion, I would like to say that as a prospective teacher, I think that this job makes a big difference to people’s lives and if you want to build a successful career, it’s important to be dedicated to teaching.
