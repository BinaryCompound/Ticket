My future life

When I'm thinking about my future, I cannot say for sure what it will be like. But I am sure that the most important things for me in the future will be my profession and family. I want to become a teacher. It seems to me it is a challenging job which has a lot of benefits and some drawbacks too. I really have a great interest in the Russian language and want to share my knowledge with others. So, I think this job makes a positive impact on people’s lives. I would like to finish my university to become a well-qualified teacher. Also, I would like to have any special training courses for teachers to improve my soft and hard skills. I believe that in the future, I will be a successful person who won’t be afraid to meet challenges, who will meet deadlines, motivate others, and complete administrative tasks. Besides, I would like to have a big family. The best holidays for me are holidays that I spend with my relatives and family. I want to believe that in the future, I will travel a lot because it is very important for me. I would meet interesting people and explore new places. This kind of holiday will increase my energy levels.
