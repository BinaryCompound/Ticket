My family tradition

The family is an important part of our life. Of course there are some unique traditions in every family. My family is rich in traditions. In my opinion, it is really important to keep up special traditions in family because it makes family members closer and can make better relationships between them. Besides, it is important to pass the tradition down to the next generation. In my family, there are some traditions. Many of them connected with some holidays. The most important is New Year. We decorate the house, cook and eat special food, dress up and watch a firework display. The main thing is to do it together. Also, we establish a tradition. This is to spend the sunday together. Sometimes my parents works overtime that’s why we don’t always have enough time to spend together this is a big drawback and i get frustrated. But we trying to spend every sunday together. We can go for a walk, we may go to the cinema. By tradition in the evening we have a dinner together. It seems to me, all these traditions have positive impacts on our family relationships. In conclusion, i would like to say that i think if you have any traditions in your family your life will be better. All traditions make a big difference to people’s lives and i think everyone should has traditions in their life.
