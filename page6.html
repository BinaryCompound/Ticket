The role of traditions in the modern world

Traditions play an important role in the modern world. It seems to me, All traditions make a big difference to people’s lives and i think everyone should has traditions in their life. All traditions have positive impacts because they helps to save cultural heritage. That’s why i believe that it is important to pass the tradition down to the next generation. Traditions make bonds between generations stronger. Every family, every culture has their own traditions, for example they may decorate the house, they can cook special food, take part in the competitions and something like that. Traditions also make family members closer and creating respect for other cultures. Besides, traditions can be sources of joy and pleasure, for example different holidays. In my opinion, it is important to keep up traditions of your family or country because it helps people to save stability and sense of belonging. In conclusion i would like to say that the role of the traditions in modern word is very important. People should have any traditions. If they have them, their life will be better.
