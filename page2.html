my university

Choosing a higher education path is always hard. You should be sure that you study something you like and you really want to make a career in this field. I am going to tell you about my university experience , and why I chose it. now i am a first year student of herzen university, at the faculty of Filology, and when I finish it I will get a bachelor’s degree. I’m on face-to-face learning and it seems to me face-to-face interactions with teachers are more effective than distance learning. Most of the teaching in my university is done through lectures, all professors and teachers are patient, knowledgeable and highly qualified and highly motivated. They read course materials and share their knowledge with us very interesting and i see that they dedicated to the profession. It seems to me choosing a university is very important part in teenagers life, and teenagers should think about it seriously.
