future inventions

Inventions are a very important part of our life. They make our life easier. One of the most popular future inventions is considered to be a time machine. This machine can move people from the past to the future and back. Besides, it will be very useful to have a device which can translate different languages. It will make communication easier and more interesting. Another invention which can be very useful is a device which will help blind people to see. It will be a huge breakthrough. Also, it will be good to have a gadget that can help to save our planet. It will remove all the harmful and toxic things from nature and make it clean. In conclusion, I would like to say that future inventions can make our life easier and more interesting. They have a positive impact on every aspect of our life.
