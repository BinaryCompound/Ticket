Who is a scientist

Science is an important part of our life. It helps us to understand this world, to solve different problems, and makes our life easier. A scientist is a person who studies science. This person can monitor and regulate experiments, he may indicate problems and something like that. Besides, their duties include analyzing the data and making discoveries. Scientists can work in different fields such as chemistry, biology, medicine, etc. There can also be drawbacks to being a scientist because it is very challenging and hard work. They also should have enough patience, they should complete difficult administrative tasks. Scientists play the greatest role in our understanding of the world, help us develop new technologies.
