personality and skills of a prospective teacher

Teaching is a rewarding job, and a prospective teacher should have special skills. Teacher duties include grading papers, homework, making a lesson plan, and of course, making communication with students better. And special skills can help a person to build a successful teaching career. In my opinion, a prospective teacher should have such soft skills as patience, time management, and communication. Very important hard skills are computer skills because part of the work a teacher can do on the computer. Besides, a prospective teacher should be open-minded and self-confident. Of course, a teacher should be organized and knowledgeable. A prospective teacher should be hardworking to complete the administrative tasks and meet deadlines. Also, it is important for a teacher to engage students in the subject, that's why a prospective teacher may be creative. All these skills and personality will help prospective teachers to motivate students, increase energy levels, and boost job performance. So, if you want to become a teacher, you should develop your personality and skills.
