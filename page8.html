A famous scientist

Science is an important part of our life. It helps us to understand this world, to solve different problems, and makes our life easier. A scientist is a person who studies science. This person can monitor and regulate experiments, he may indicate problems and something like that. Besides, these people analyze the data and make discoveries and of course make a positive impact on our life. There are a lot of different scientists, and I would like to tell you about Stephen Hawking. He was born in 1942. He was an English theoretical physicist, cosmologist, and author. He made the greatest contributions to our understanding of the universe through his work on black holes. He published his work in the forms of books and essays. His most famous work is considered to be A Brief History Time. This book became an international bestseller and sold over 10 million copies in 20 years.
